Melissa's Zoo Animals

At our zoo, we have a wonderful group of cute, curious animals. Come by and get to know their spunky personalities!

Bears

  • Ollie
  • Mona

Ollie and Mona are a Grizzly mom and cub pair from Alaska. We are really excited to have them! You can learn more about Grizzly Bears here.

Giraffes

  • Frankie
  • Coconut

Our 10 year old giraffe Frankie recently gave birth to Coconut, her first daughter. Come and watch Coconut take her first steps! Learn more about giraffes here.

Lions

  • Mella
  • Karl

Mella and Karl arrived 3 years ago from sub-Saharan Africa. Karl is shy and rarely comes outside, but you can usually find Mella basking in the sun, waiting for you to come by so she can say hello. You can learn more about lions here.

Monkeys

  • Cookie
  • Earl
  • Banana Pudding

Cookie, Earl and Banana Pudding are our zoo's resident trouble makers. They are tons of fun to watch, but don't get too close or they might hit you with a piece of fruit! You can learn more about monkeys here.

Alligators

  • Wren
  • Aspen
  • Mika

Wren, Aspen and Mika are rarely out of the water, but you can occasionally catch them creeping across the swamp if you watch really closely. To get a good look at them, come by during feeding time. You can learn more about alligators here.
